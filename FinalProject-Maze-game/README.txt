Instruction:

Open the game and set to Full HD then open scene MainMenu and click play on unity. Once the game is started you may view instruction, credit or start the game.

Control:
Use WASD or left thumb stick of XBox controller to move ball.

Goal:
Control the ball to pass the finishing line and land on red board in as little time as possible with following two requirements:
1. Not falling on the purple ground.
2. Collecting all pickups by hitting them with limited speed(IMPORTANT: Pickups will not be collcted if hit by player too fast).

To restart the game click the exit botton on top right and start the game again.
